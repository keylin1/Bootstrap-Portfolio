Marshmellowdrama

Stopmotion project story about a dramatic marshmallow. What will happen when he finally confesses his love to his childhood friend?

A Summary

The story begins with a marshmallow fondly reminiscing about the days of its youth and a vow made with a childhood companion. Years later, the marshmallow, now grown, marks the appointed day for the fulfillment of that promise. With a bouquet of roses in hand, anxiety grips him as he awaits her arrival, giving himself a reassuring pep talk.

Upon entering her home, he is met with the sight of his childhood friend alongside her current boyfriend. Shock overwhelms him, causing him to drop the bouquet dramatically. Collapsing onto her apartment floor, he succumbs to a torrent of tears as his cherished aspirations shatter. Despite her concern, he retreats into despair and rolls off into the void.
